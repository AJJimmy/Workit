It's Time to Work it Out!

Welcome to workit, where we prioritize the comprehensive well-being of women through specialized physiotherapy. I am Aanuoluwapo Adejimi, BMR(PT), a seasoned women’s health physiotherapist, and I am excited to guide you on your journey to optimal health.With a profound commitment to women’s wellness, I bring over 2 years of experience in addressing a spectrum of issues unique to women. From pelvic floor concerns and prenatal care to postnatal rehabilitation, my practice is grounded in evidence-based approaches tailored to meet your specific needs.

At workit, we understand the nuanced nature of women’s health. Our mission is to empower you through personalized care plans that consider not only the physical aspects but also the emotional and mental dimensions of your well-being. We believe in fostering a supportive environment where you can openly discuss your concerns and actively participate in your healing process.

Our services extend beyond traditional physiotherapy, encompassing education and guidance on lifestyle modifications to promote lasting wellness. Whether you are navigating pregnancy, recovering from childbirth, or managing pelvic health concerns, we are here to provide compassionate care and assist you in reclaiming your strength and confidence.

Explore our website to learn more about the services we offer, testimonials from satisfied clients, and valuable resources to support your health journey. Take a proactive step towards a healthier you, and let workit be your partner in women’s health physiotherapy.
